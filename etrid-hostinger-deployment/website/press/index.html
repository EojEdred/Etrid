Official Statements

Company Boilerplate

ËTRID is a next-generation blockchain protocol designed to enable seamless cross-chain interoperability. Through its innovative Partition Burst Chain architecture and Ascending Scale of Finality (ASF) consensus mechanism, ËTRID connects 13 major blockchain networks including Ethereum, Bitcoin, Solana, and Binance Smart Chain. The protocol provides developers with unified APIs to build cross-chain applications while maintaining high performance with 5-second block times and 15-second finality. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, ËTRID is backed by a global community of validators, developers, and stakeholders committed to building the multichain future.

Short Description

ËTRID is a high-performance blockchain protocol enabling cross-chain interoperability across 13 major networks. With 5-second block times and innovative Partition Burst Chain architecture, ËTRID empowers developers to build seamless multichain applications.
