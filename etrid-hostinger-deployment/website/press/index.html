Official Statements

Company Boilerplate

√ãTRID is a next-generation blockchain protocol designed to enable seamless cross-chain interoperability. Through its innovative Partition Burst Chain architecture and Ascending Scale of Finality (ASF) consensus mechanism, √ãTRID connects 13 major blockchain networks including Ethereum, Bitcoin, Solana, and Binance Smart Chain. The protocol provides developers with unified APIs to build cross-chain applications while maintaining high performance with 5-second block times and 15-second finality. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, √ãTRID is backed by a global community of validators, developers, and stakeholders committed to building the multichain future.
