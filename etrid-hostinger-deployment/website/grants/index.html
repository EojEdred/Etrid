Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for grants?

Anyone building on √ãTRID can apply - from solo developers to established teams. We evaluate based on technical merit, team capability, and potential impact.

How long does the application process take?

Initial review takes 2 weeks. Complete process from application to funding typically takes 4-6 weeks depending on the grant tier.

Are grants paid in √âTR tokens or stablecoins?

Grants are paid in a mix of √âTR and stablecoins (USDC/USDT) based on recipient preference. We offer flexible payment terms to minimize volatility risk.

What happens if we don't meet milestones?

We work with teams to adjust timelines and milestones if needed. Open communication is key. Persistent delays may result in grant suspension pending reassessment.

Do you take equity or tokens?

Grants are non-dilutive - we don't take equity. For strategic grants, we may request token allocation for community distribution, but this is negotiable.
