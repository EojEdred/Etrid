What is Consensus Day?

An annual on-chain governance event where the ËTRID community makes major protocol decisions

Annual Event

Occurs every December 1st at 00:00 UTC for 22 hours

Stake-Weighted

Voting power based on ÉTR stake and holding duration

Fiscal Policy

Vote on annual inflation rate (0-5% cap)

Protocol Upgrades

Approve major network upgrades and changes

The Four Phases

Consensus Day runs for 22 hours across four distinct phases

1

Registration Phase

6 hours • 00:00 - 06:00 UTC

Submit proposals, lock stakes, and register participation for voting

  • Proposal submission (≥10,000 ÉTR required)
  • Stake locking for voting power calculation
  • Validator registration and role confirmation
2

Voting Phase

12 hours • 06:00 - 18:00 UTC

Cast votes on all active proposals using ASF-weighted voting power

  • Vote on fiscal policy (inflation rate 0-5%)
  • Approve protocol upgrades (60-100% approval required)
  • Select 9 Decentralized Directors
3

Minting Phase

3 hours • 18:00 - 21:00 UTC

Execute approved inflation rate and mint new ÉTR tokens

  • Calculate total new supply based on voting results
  • Mint new ÉTR tokens to treasury
  • Prepare distribution allocations
4

Distribution Phase

1 hour • 21:00 - 22:00 UTC

Automatically distribute minted tokens to Foundation, Validators, and Voters

  • 40% → Foundation Treasury (development, operations)
  • 40% → Validator Rewards (network security)
  • 20% → Distribution Pay (voter participation rewards)
