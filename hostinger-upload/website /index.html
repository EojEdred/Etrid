ASF Voting Power

Ascending Scale of Finality formula rewards long-term commitment with enhanced voting power.

Voting Power Formula
√(Stake × Coinage)
Where Coinage = Days Held × Amount
This formula encourages long-term participation and rewards committed community members with proportionally more influence.

Stake-Weighted Voting

Your stake is reserved during voting periods to ensure committed participation
